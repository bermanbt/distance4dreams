2011

Elijah is a four-year-old who loves Spongebob, Toy Story, Iron Man, Spiderman, and Scooby Doo. His favorite past times are playing with blocks and using his imagination. His favorite color is blue, and his mom says that being a boy, Elijah is entertained by dirt very easily, too!

When the D4D team visited Elijah at Give Kids the World, he had just returned from a day at Universal Studios. Over cookies and punch, Elijah showed everyone how to do the "Hulk Smash" and gave every member of D4D a high-five as a "Thank You" for granting his wish. Elijah and his family are extremely appreciative that Distance 4 Dreams made Elijah’s dream of going to Disney World come true.
