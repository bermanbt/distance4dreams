2010

Paige is in the 4th grade, and full of energy and smiles. Her energy and excitement was perfect for the Distance 4 Dreams Minnie 5k as she started the race and cheered on all of the participants.

Paige loves animals, and some of her favorites are horses, rabbits, and snakes (because of their scaly skin). Her favorite holiday is Halloween because she loves getting together with friends and family, trading candy, dressing up, and all of the scary things that come with the holiday. Fall is her favorite season, not only because of Halloween, but because of the reds, oranges, and yellows all around. Paige loves to cook and watch the Food Network and would someday like to be a chef.

Paige said she is very excited for her family’s trip to Disney World! The flight to Disney World will be the first time she and her sister have flown; she is looking forward to a very exciting experience!

Paige said "I would like to tell you that I’m very grateful for all of the people who are sponsoring me and my Wish."
