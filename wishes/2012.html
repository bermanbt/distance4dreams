2012

Colton is a twelve-year-old who loves SpongeBob Square Pants and Phineas and Ferb. He enjoys dressing up like Woody from Toy Story for Halloween and his is favorite color is orange.

Colton and his family traveled to Give Kids the World Village in December of 2011. Members of Distance 4 Dreams were able to meet in February after his trip to hear all about his it! His family brought a slideshow of photos from their week in Florida and they told D4D about some of the best parts of the trip, including the limo ride, meeting all the characters, and Colton’s first ride on a carousel! Colton and his family seemed to have had a great vacation together.
